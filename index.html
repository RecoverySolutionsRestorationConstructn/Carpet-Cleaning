Carpet Cleaning

When dirt, mud and grime overtake your carpets, trust the carpet cleaning specialists at Recovery Solutions to help.

With pets, guests and everyday living, it’s hard to know what bacteria lives in your carpets and furniture. From accidents and spills to routine maintenance, our carpet cleaning technicians offer professional carpet cleaning and upholstery restoration solutions.

Read Post

Unlike DIY carpet cleaning trends that can leave your carpets soaked, professional carpet cleaning provides lasting results using hot water extraction techniques, also known as steam cleaning. Extracting dirt and soil from your carpet, our process will remove the majority of the moisture in your carpet, leaving it only slightly damp to the touch and looking like new.

While it’s typical to find a professional carpet cleaning company for emergency spills or upcoming parties, scheduling a deep carpet cleaning once a year is recommended to increase your carpet’s life expectancy by at least 50% while creating a healthier environment at home.
